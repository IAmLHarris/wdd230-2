Purpose

The Santiago Chamber of Commerce website is intended to be the place new and existing members go to be informed about the buisiness development happening to champion Santiago in buisiness. We will provide event calendars, physical meeting times, and online sign ups.

Audience

The audience of our website are small and large buisiness owners in Santiago. The typical small buisiness owner are usually in their mid 20's to late 60's, usually with typical income. Most would access on smaller devices. The usual large buisiness owner is in their late 40's to late 60's, with a higher income. This part of our audience would access on a larger device, such as a desktop.
